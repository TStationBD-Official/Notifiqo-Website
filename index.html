Privacy Policy

How we protect your data — the same policy shown inside the app.

1. Data Collection

Notifiqo captures notification content (title, body, sender, and application name) to provide you with a searchable history. This data is primarily stored locally on your device.

2. Media & Attachments

Images and file attachments (like PDFs) from notifications are stored strictly in your app's private internal storage. We do not upload these files to any external cloud storage or servers.

3. Cloud Synchronization

If you choose to enable Cloud Sync and sign in with Google, your notification text history is securely synced to Firebase Cloud Firestore. This allows you to access your history on other devices. You can disable this at any time in Settings.

4. Sharing Features

When you explicitly share your notifications with another user via the "Looking Up" feature, that user will be able to view your synced notification history in real-time. You maintain full control and can revoke access at any time.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and Firebase security protocols to protect your synced data. Local data is protected by the Android operating system's app isolation security.

6. User Rights

You have the right to clear your local cache or permanently delete all your data (both local and cloud) at any time using the "Reset All Data" option in Settings, or the account deletion options described below.

Delete Your Account & Data

You're always in control of your data. Here's how to permanently delete your Notifiqo account and everything associated with it.

1

In-app (fastest)

Open Notifiqo → Settings → sign in with the same Google account → tap Delete Account under your profile → confirm. This deletes your cloud data, local data, and your Notifiqo account immediately.

2

By email request

Don't have the app installed anymore? Email support.notifiqo@gmail.com from the Google account you used to sign in, with the subject "Delete My Notifiqo Account". We process these requests within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • All notifications, automation rules, and shipment records synced to the cloud (Firebase Firestore)
  • All notification history and settings stored locally on your device
  • Your Google-linked Notifiqo account itself (Firebase Authentication)
  • Any encrypted backups you've stored via Google Drive backup (deleted from your own Drive by removing the backup files, or via Google Drive directly)
